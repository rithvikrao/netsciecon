Instructions for Accessing Virtual Space (Guests)

Dear guests,

The website and program are live at netsciecon.org. This message contains important instructions for using the virtual conference space, as well as links for accessing the platform. Please contact the organizers if you would like the password.

Most important points:

To attend the conference, you MUST access the conference through Google Chrome. Other browsers cause serious unexpected problems, especially when presenting!

Once logged in, zoom in and out by using your mouse's scroll feature. Pan by clicking and dragging. Then explore!

To change rooms, click on the "show room" icon in the upper right corner to see a list of rooms, and join a room by clicking on its name.

Whenever you need help, find people with yellow badges. These conference staff members will help you with whatever you need.

You can refer to this PDF file for more detailed facts about the platform and conference. A video walkthrough for logging in is here if you need it. Please let us know if you have any questions! We are looking forward to seeing you at the conference!

Best wishes,
